Let’s Discover Pohnpei Together

Life on Pohnpei is about more than just business—it’s about community, culture, and the simple joys that make our island home truly special. As members and future members of the Chamber of Commerce, we invite you to explore the places, tastes, and experiences that bring us together and remind us why we do what we do. From unforgettable sites to the tradition of sakau, these 8 highlights capture the spirit of enjoying life while growing together—both as individuals and as a thriving local economy.
