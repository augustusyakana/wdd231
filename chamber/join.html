Partnership Levels

NP (Non-Profit) Partnership – Free

Exclusively for non-profit organizations looking to engage with the local business community and expand their reach.

NP (non-profit) BENEFITS

✅ Business listing in the online chamber directory
✅ Access to networking events and community forums
✅ Eligibility for chamber grants and funding opportunities
✅ Promotion of events and initiatives on chamber platforms
✅ Discounted advertising in chamber publications

Close

Bronze Partnership – $150/year

Great for small businesses and startups looking to establish a presence in the community.

BRONZE BENEFITS

✅ Business listing in the online chamber directory
✅ Access to networking events and educational workshops
✅ Member-exclusive discounts on chamber programs and advertising
✅ Ribbon-cutting ceremony for new businesses
✅ Inclusion in the quarterly chamber newsletter

Close

Silver Membership – $350/year

Designed for growing businesses that want increased exposure and additional benefits.

SILVER BENEFITS

Includes all Bronze Membership benefits, plus:
✅ Priority placement in the online chamber directory
✅ Two free featured social media promotions per year
✅ Discounted booth space at chamber expos and trade shows
✅ Access to small business mentorship programs
✅ Opportunity to sponsor chamber events at a discounted rate

Close

Gold Partnership – $750/year

The premium membership for businesses looking for leadership opportunities and maximum visibility.

GOLD BENEFITS

Includes all Silver Membership benefits, plus:
✅ Featured spotlight in the chamber newsletter and website
✅ Free booth space at chamber expos and trade shows
✅ VIP access to exclusive networking and leadership events
✅ Complimentary advertising in chamber newsletters and social media
✅ Speaking opportunities at chamber-hosted events

Close
