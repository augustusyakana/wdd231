Membership Levels

Basic Membership

A practical starting point for small businesses or community employers. Basic gives you the essentials to start posting jobs and reviewing local applicants.

Basic Membership Cost & Benefits

$15/month

  • Post up to 10 active job listings
  • View up to 25 applicant profiles per month
  • Access to a basic job management dashboard
  • Standard email support
  • Listings appear in standard search results

Premium Membership

Ideal for businesses with regular hiring needs. Premium boosts your visibility and provides more tools to help you manage job posts and applicants efficiently.

Premium Membership Cost & Benefits

$35/month

  • Post up to 30 active job listings
  • View up to 100 applicant profiles per month
  • Listings receive priority placement in job search results
  • Company logo included on listings
  • Access to applicant sorting tools
  • Email and chat support during business hours

Elite Membership

Designed for larger organizations, agencies, or frequent recruiters. Elite offers the highest visibility, early feature access, and priority support.

Elite Membership Cost & Benefits

$75/month

  • Post up to 75 active job listings
  • View up to 300 applicant profiles per month
  • Listings appear in featured sections
  • Full company branding on job pages
  • Early access to AI-powered candidate matching (in development)
  • Monthly summary reports (basic insights)
  • Priority support via email and chat
